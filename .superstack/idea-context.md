# Solana Digital Product Landscape

## Recommended Idea

Build a marketplace for **licensed creator collectible packs on Solana**, starting with a narrower wedge:

- **Sticker / emote packs for creators, communities, and fan clubs**
- Then expand into **music clips, short videos, bonus art, and gated fan bundles**

This is narrower and less crowded than generic NFT marketplaces, while still matching Solana's strengths: low-cost mass minting, tradable ownership, resale royalties, and easy secondary liquidity.

## Practical Takeaway

Avoid building:

- another generic NFT marketplace
- another 1/1 art venue
- another trader-first aggregator

Start with:

- creator sticker/emote packs
- meme/community packs
- music or video fan packs
- collectible bundles that unlock perks or gated channels

That is where Solana's low fees and tradability change the product itself instead of just adding crypto branding.
